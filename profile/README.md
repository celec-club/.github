# CELEC

**The leading tech club in Algeria, fostering innovation in electronics, IoT, and robotics.**

Founded in **1984**, CELEC has been dedicated to **learning, teaching, and advancing technology** for the new generation of engineers and tech enthusiasts in Algeria.

## Our Mission

At CELEC, we strive to:

* Empower students and professionals with practical knowledge in **electronics, IoT, and robotics**.
* Promote **innovation, creativity, and hands-on experience** through projects and workshops.
* Build a **community of tech enthusiasts** who are eager to learn and share knowledge.

## Leadership

Currently led by **Yasser Fedsi**, Head of the IT Department, who oversees all digital and technological initiatives within the organization.
